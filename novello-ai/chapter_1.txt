 Chapter 1 – The Death of Smart
It was in a small room, in a small town, where the idea first took shape, an idea that would change the world forever. The day was dull and grey, but inside the room it was bright with excitement.

The two men sat at the table, looking over the figures. "So you're saying we can make 50x returns on this product?" asked Mike, a tall man with a shock of dark hair and piercing blue eyes.

"Exactly," replied his friend, John, a shorter man with a slight build and a quiet manner. "The margins are incredible."

"But how do we do it?" Mike asked.

John smiled. "It's simple. We're going to leverage technology like never before." He explained the plan.

"Are you sure this will work?"

"I'm confident," John said, his voice steady.
